English and Hindi hits compared over the decades


This page shows the comparison between hindi and english hits in the 90s in terms of danceability and acousticness.

Spotify gives a acousticness score to each track to denote the confidence level of acoustic elements in a track. It is a number between 0 and 1 with a more "acoustic" track having a higher value.

Used here is a kde plot for denoting the difference in the measures of hindi and english tracks in a single plot
