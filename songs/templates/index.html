Songs Then and Now

English and Hindi hits compared over the decades


All the analysis across this project have been done across top hindi and english songs across from decades 80s to 2010s . The tracks from curated playlists for each decade have been gathered for analysis through Spotify APIs.

For English, the Billboard hits for each decade have been analysed. This page shows the break-up of the country of origin of the billboard hits across each decade. As is apparent, the majority of the Billboard hits are from artists in the USA followed by GB (Great Britain or UK) . During the 80s and 90s, CA (Canada) also made it's presence felt with a few contributions to the Billboard top tracks.
